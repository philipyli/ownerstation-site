What is SlideDeck.js?

SlideDeck.js lets you organize any type of content into a beautiful, customizable and user-friendly slideshow.

Simply put, it's a better way to present content on the web.

    There are lots of content sliders out there, but SlideDeck.js offers some unique features that many of them lack, such as:

    • Handling mixed content
    • Dual-axis sliding
    • Touchscreen compatible
    • PRO support team*

  • Mixed Content Types

    Many sliders only accommodate images, or force you to use the same template for each slide.

    SlideDeck.js can take just about anything you throw at it, letting you focus on creating engaging content, instead of wasting time and coming up with clever hacks and workarounds.

  • Vertical Slides

    Why should you only move in one direction? SlideDeck.js lets your users explore your content both horizontally and vertically within one presentation.
